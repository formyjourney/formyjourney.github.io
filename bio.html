About Me

Before becoming a UX designer, I studied International Studies at Ramapo College and worked for a number of years as an administrative assistant. I’ve always been a relentless problem solver so I found my love of understanding customers’ needs and then creating great experiences through my work. More so, I realized I had a knack for this kind of work when I edited and published the weekly company newsletter. I learned basic graphic design, copywriting, and typography by myself. I loved it so much that after I took a step back in my career, I decided to elevate my skills and came across UX design. I enrolled at Bloc where I learned more about user research and visual design.

I believe design can answer problems that are more human than today. With my background in business, customer empathy, and design, I look forward to creating meaningful and memorable experiences for all.
